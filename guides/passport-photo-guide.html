📐 Understanding Head Size Requirements

70-80% of photo

What is "Head Size"?

The distance from the top of your head (crown) to the bottom of your chin should take up 70-80% of the total photo height. Our tool automatically checks this for you!

  • Too small: Face not visible enough → Rejected
  • Too large: Face too close → Rejected
  • Just right: Our tool ensures perfect positioning

Take Your Photo

Follow these tips for a perfect original photo:

  • Stand against a plain white or light-colored wall
  • Use natural daylight or bright, even lighting
  • Face forward, both eyes open, mouth closed (no smile)
  • No hats, sunglasses, or hair covering face
  • Take photo at eye level (not from above or below)

✨ Advanced Tips for Perfect Photos

Lighting

Use natural daylight. Avoid shadows on face or background.

Glasses

Remove if possible. If worn, ensure no glare.

Expression

Neutral face, mouth closed, both eyes open.

Head Position

Face forward, centered, not tilted.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Can I take passport photo with my phone?

A: Yes! Modern phone cameras (12MP+) are perfect. Just follow our lighting and background tips.

Q: Do I need to smile?

A: No. Most countries require neutral expression with mouth closed. USA and UK specifically prohibit smiling.

Q: Can I wear glasses?

A: USA prohibits glasses entirely. UK allows only with medical note. Best to remove if possible.

Q: What background color do I need?

A: White for USA, India, Canada. Cream/light grey for UK, Schengen. Our tool handles both.

Q: How recent should my photo be?

A: Taken within last 6 months. You should look the same as current appearance.

Q: Can I use the same photo for visa and passport?

A: Requirements may differ. Always check specific country's visa photo rules.
