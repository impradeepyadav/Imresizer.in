💡 Tips for Maximum Compression

  • Scan at lower DPI: If creating new scans, use 150 DPI instead of 300 DPI
  • Convert to black & white: For text documents, grayscale compresses smaller than color
  • Remove unnecessary pages: Extract only needed pages before compressing
  • Try different levels: Start with Medium, if too large try High
  • Check text readability: Ensure text remains clear after compression

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How much can I reduce PDF size?

A: With High compression, you can reduce size by 70-80%. A 10MB PDF can become 2-3MB.

Q: Will compression affect quality?

A: Some quality loss occurs, especially with High compression. Medium compression offers good balance.

Q: What's the best compression for email?

A> High compression works best for email. Aim to keep files under 10-15MB for reliable sending.

Q: Can I compress password-protected PDFs?

A: No, PDFs must be unlocked. Remove password protection before compressing.

Q: Is there a file size limit?

A> No strict limit, but very large files (200MB+) may take longer to process in browser.

Q: Do I need to create an account?

A> No registration needed. Free and instant.

Q: Can I compress multiple PDFs at once?

A> Currently, our tool handles one PDF at a time for best quality control.
