🎚️ Understanding DPI (Quality Settings)

72 DPI
Web / Social Media

Small file size (100-300KB per page)
Good for screens, email

150 DPI
Standard Quality

Balance of quality & size (300-800KB per page)
Recommended for most uses

300 DPI
Print Quality

High quality (1-3MB per page)
Best for printing

📊 Quality Settings Comparison

Quality DPI File Size (per page) Best For

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How many pages can I convert at once?

A: There's no fixed limit. 50-100 pages work well. Very large PDFs (200+ pages) may take longer to process in browser.

Q: Will the text be readable in JPG images?

A: Yes, especially at 150 DPI or higher. For text-heavy documents, Medium or High quality is recommended.

Q: Can I convert only specific pages?

A: Currently, the tool converts all pages. For selective pages, consider splitting PDF first.

Q: What's the difference between 72 DPI and 300 DPI?

A> 72 DPI is for screens (web, social media). 300 DPI is for printing. Higher DPI = larger file size and better quality.

Q: Can I convert password-protected PDFs?

A: No, PDFs must be unlocked. Remove password protection before uploading.

Q: Do I need to create an account?

A> No registration needed. Free and instant.

Q: Can I get images in PNG format instead?

A> Yes! Our tool also supports PNG output. Select PNG option if you need transparency or sharper text.
