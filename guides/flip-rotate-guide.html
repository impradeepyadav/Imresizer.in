💡 Pro Tips

  • Multiple clicks: Each click rotates 90°. Four clicks bring you back to start.
  • Combine operations: You can rotate then flip for complex corrections.
  • Check text: If text appears backwards, use Flip Horizontal.
  • Before downloading: Always preview to ensure correct orientation.
  • Original is preserved: Tool doesn't save your original - download only.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I rotate an image 180 degrees?

A: Click the Rotate Right button twice. Each click rotates 90°, so two clicks give you 180°.

Q: What's the difference between flip and rotate?

A: Rotate turns the image (like spinning a photo). Flip creates a mirror image (like looking in a mirror).

Q: Why does my selfie have text backwards?

A> Front cameras show a mirror image. Use Flip Horizontal to correct it.

Q: Will rotating reduce image quality?

A> No, rotating and flipping are lossless operations. Quality remains the same.

Q: Can I rotate in small increments like 5°?

A> Our tool rotates in 90° steps. For small adjustments, use the crop tool's rotate feature.

Q: Do I need to create an account?

A> No registration needed. Free and instant.
