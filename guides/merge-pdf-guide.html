❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How many PDFs can I merge at once?

A: There's no fixed limit. You can merge 10-15 PDFs at once. Performance depends on file sizes and your device.

Q: Will the quality of my PDFs decrease?

A: No, we preserve original quality. Pages are combined without any compression or quality loss.

Q: Can I merge password-protected PDFs?
