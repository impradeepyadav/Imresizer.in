⚠️ Important Tips for Perfect Background

Tip Why It Matters
Start with good original photo Better contrast = better background removal
Avoid flyaway hair Tidy hair makes edge refinement easier
Check at 100% zoom Ensure no leftover original background pixels
Use feathering for hair Creates natural transition around hair

Problem Solution

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Can I change background to any color?

A: Yes! You can choose from preset colors (white, cream, grey, light blue) or enter any custom HEX color code for specific requirements.

Q: Does this work for group photos?

A: The tool works best for single person passport/visa photos. For group photos, manual editing may be needed.

Q: What if my photo has a complex background?

A> The AI handles most backgrounds well. For very complex backgrounds, use manual brush tool to refine.

Q: Is white or cream background better?

A> Depends on your country: USA/India need white, UK/Europe need cream/light grey. Check requirements first.

Q: Can I use this for exam photos?

A> Yes! Most exams require white background. Our tool works perfectly for SSC, UPSC, Banking photos.

Q: Do I need to crop first or change background first?

A> Change background first, then use our crop tool for exact dimensions. Order doesn't matter significantly.
