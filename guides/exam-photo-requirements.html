✅ Common Requirements Across All Exams

  • Photo Background: White or very light grey - no patterns, no shadows
  • Photo Age: Taken within last 3-6 months (varies by exam)
  • Expression: Neutral face, both eyes open, mouth closed (no smile)
  • Accessories: No hats, no sunglasses (prescription glasses allowed if no glare)
  • Format: JPG/JPEG for most exams (GATE accepts PNG)
  • Head Position: Face forward, centered, covering 70-80% of photo

❌ Top 10 Reasons for Photo Rejection

  • Wrong file size: Photo over 50KB when limit is 20KB
  • Incorrect dimensions: Not following exact cm or pixel requirements
  • Wrong background: Using colored, patterned, or dark background
  • Face covered: Hair over face, shadows, or turned head
  • Old photo: Photo doesn't match current appearance
  • Smiling: Most exams require neutral expression
  • Glasses glare: Reflection on glasses hiding eyes
  • Red eyes: Flash reflection in eyes
  • Wrong format: Uploading PNG when JPG is required
  • Head too small/large: Not meeting 70-80% rule

📱 Mobile Photo Tips

Taking exam photos with phone? Follow these tips:

  • Use rear camera: Better quality than front camera
  • Clean lens: Wipe camera lens before taking photo
  • Good lighting: Natural daylight works best
  • Steady hands: Rest phone on something or use tripod
  • Distance: Hold phone at arm's length, face filling frame

A: Yes, but you may need to resize differently. Save the original and create copies with required dimensions using our Resizer.

Q: What if my photo is exactly 50KB but requirement says 20KB?

A: Use our Image Compressor with target 20KB. The tool will reduce size while maintaining quality.

Q: Can I smile in exam photos?

A: Most exams require neutral expression with mouth closed. SSC, UPSC, Banking all specify no smiling.

A: Within last 3-6 months. UPSC specifically requires "within 30 days" for some applications.

A: Better to avoid. If necessary, ensure no glare and frames don't cover eyes. Some exams explicitly prohibit glasses.
