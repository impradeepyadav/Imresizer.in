📸 JPG vs PNG for PDF Conversion

JPG Format

  • ✅ Smaller file size
  • ✅ Good for photos & documents
  • ⚠️ No transparency
  • ✅ Best for web sharing

PNG Format

  • ✅ Larger file size
  • ✅ Preserves sharp text
  • ✅ Supports transparency
  • ✅ Best for diagrams & charts

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How many pages can I convert at once?

A: There's no fixed limit, but 50-100 pages work well. Very large PDFs may take longer to process in browser.

Q: Will the text in my PDF be readable in images?

A: Yes, especially if you use PNG format and 150+ DPI. PNG preserves text sharpness better than JPG.

Q: Can I convert only specific pages?

A: Currently, the tool converts all pages. For selective pages, consider splitting PDF first.

Q: What's the difference between 72 DPI and 300 DPI?

A> 72 DPI is for screens (web, social media). 300 DPI is for printing. Higher DPI = larger file size.

Q: Can I convert password-protected PDFs?

A: No, PDFs must be unlocked. Remove password protection before uploading.

Q: Do I need to create an account?
