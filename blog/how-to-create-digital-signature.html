✍️ Method 1: Draw Your Signature

Draw using mouse or touch screen

The most natural way to create a signature that looks like your handwritten signature.

  1. Select "Draw Signature" tab – This opens the drawing canvas.
  2. Adjust settings – Choose your preferred pen color, background color, and pen size using the sliders (1px to 10px).
  3. Draw your signature – Use your mouse (on desktop) or finger/stylus (on mobile) to draw directly on the canvas.
  4. Use controls – "Undo" to remove the last stroke or "Clear All" to start fresh.
  5. Generate – Click "Generate Signature" when satisfied, then download as PNG or JPG.
✨ Pro Tip: For best results, use a stylus on mobile or draw slowly. Higher pen size values create thicker lines. If your signature looks jagged, increase the smoothing value for smoother curves.

🔤 Method 2: Type Your Signature

Type using various fonts

Perfect for creating clean, professional signatures using various fonts.

  1. Select "Type Signature" tab – This opens the text input panel.
  2. Enter your name – Type your full name as you want it to appear.
  3. Choose font style – Select from 10+ professionally curated fonts including elegant scripts (Brush Script, Pacifico), formal serifs (Times New Roman, Georgia), and clean sans-serifs (Arial, Verdana).
  4. Adjust size and color – Pick the appropriate font size and text color.
  5. Preview – See real-time preview of your signature on the canvas below.
  6. Generate – Click "Generate & Save" to create your signature.
🔤 Font Selection Tips: For official documents, choose professional fonts like Arial, Times New Roman, or Georgia. For a personal touch, Brush Script or Pacifico mimics handwriting beautifully.

📤 Method 3: Upload Your Signature

Upload scanned signature image

Already have a scanned image of your signature? Upload it and convert to a clean digital signature.

  1. Select "Upload Signature" tab – This opens the upload area.
  2. Upload image – Click or drag & drop your signature image (PNG, JPG, GIF up to 5MB).
  3. Automatic processing – The tool automatically centers and resizes your signature to fit the canvas.
  4. Review – Check if the signature looks good. You can remove and re-upload if needed.
  5. Save – Click "Save as Signature" to process and download.
📸 Upload Best Practices: For best results, upload an image with a clean white background and dark ink. The tool works best with high-contrast images.

📥 Download Options: PNG vs JPG

RECOMMENDED

PNG (Portable Network Graphics)

  • ✅ Supports transparent background
  • ✅ Ideal for overlaying on documents, PDFs, or any colored background
  • ✅ No white box around signature
  • ✅ Best for professional use

JPG (JPEG)

  • ⚠️ Always has white background
  • ✅ Smaller file size
  • ⚠️ Less versatile
  • ✅ Suitable for basic use

Recommendation: Always choose PNG for official documents to maintain a professional look without background artifacts.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Can I use this signature for legal documents?

A: Yes, digital signatures created with our tool are widely accepted for most online forms, applications, and digital documents. However, for legally binding contracts, check specific requirements – some may require DSC (Digital Signature Certificate).

Q: What file format is best for signatures?

A: PNG format is recommended as it supports transparency, allowing your signature to be placed on any colored background without a white box. JPG adds a white background but has smaller file size.

Q: Can I resize my signature after creating it?

A: Absolutely! After downloading, use our main Image Resizer tool to adjust dimensions to exactly match exam requirements (like 4.0 x 2.0 cm for SSC).
