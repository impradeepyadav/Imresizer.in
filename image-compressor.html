Compress Image to Exact KB – How It Works

Our image compressor uses a smart binary‑search algorithm to adjust the image quality until the file size hits your target – whether it's 20KB, 50KB, 100KB, or any number you enter. Why Exact KB Compression Matters

Many online forms – especially for government exams like SSC, UPSC, Banking, and Railways – have strict file size limits (e.g., “photo must be between 20KB and 50KB”). Our tool ensures your image falls exactly within those limits, preventing rejection due to “file too large” or “file too small” errors.

Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate is the compression?

The tool aims for your target KB and typically achieves it within 1‑2KB. If your target is very low (e.g., 5KB), it may be slightly above, but we always provide the closest possible result.

Can I compress PNG files?

Yes, you can upload PNG. For PNG, we adjust the image dimensions and then save as PNG, which may result in larger files than JPG. For smaller sizes, choose JPG.

What happens if I don't enter a target KB?

If you leave the field empty, the tool compresses at 92% quality without targeting a specific size – useful when you only want to resize.

Is my data safe?

Absolutely. Everything runs in your browser. Your images are never uploaded to any server. You can even disconnect from the internet after the page loads – the tool will still work.

How to make photo under 50KB?

Upload your photo, enter 50 in Target Size, click Compress. Our algorithm automatically compresses the photo to your target size or the closest possible.
