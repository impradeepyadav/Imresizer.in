🎯 Why Exact File Size Matters

Most government exam portals (SSC, UPSC, Banking, Railways) require photos between 20KB-50KB. If your photo is even 1KB larger, the system rejects it. Our guide shows you how to hit the target every time.

💡 Pro Tips for Perfect Compression

🎯 For 20KB Target (SSC, Banking, Railways)

  • Start with right dimensions: First resize to exam specs (like 4x2 cm or 520x260 pixels) using our Image Resizer
  • Use JPG format: JPG compresses smaller than PNG while maintaining quality
  • Avoid text/watermarks: Text increases file size unnecessarily
  • If too large: Reduce dimensions slightly (by 5-10%) before compressing

📸 For 50KB Target (UPSC, NEET)

  • Higher quality allowed: Can maintain better image quality
  • PNG works: If you need transparency or crisp text
  • Dimension priority: Keep required dimensions, adjust quality

🌐 For 100KB Target (Websites, Blogs)

  • Balance quality & size: 100KB gives good quality for most web uses
  • Consider WEBP: Modern format that compresses better than JPG
  • Great for product images: Detailed photos look good at this size
