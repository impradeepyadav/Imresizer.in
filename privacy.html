Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 28, 2026

1. Our Privacy-First Approach

At ImResizer, we believe privacy is a fundamental right. That's why we built our tool to work entirely within your browser. You don't need to create an account, log in, or trust us with your personal data. All image processing happens locally on your device.

2. Information We Collect

We do NOT collect:

  • Personal information (names, email addresses, phone numbers)
  • User accounts or login credentials
  • Uploaded images or documents
  • Payment or billing information
  • Any data that can identify you personally

We may collect anonymous usage data:

  • Internet Protocol (IP) address (anonymized and truncated)
  • Browser type and version
  • Operating system
  • Date and time of visit
  • Referring website
  • Basic page interaction data (page views, clicks on buttons)

This information is used only for analytics, improving our service, and detecting technical issues. It cannot be linked to you personally and is processed in accordance with applicable data protection laws.

3. Cookies and Local Storage

ImResizer uses minimal cookies for essential functions and analytics:

  • Essential cookies: For basic website functionality and maintaining your preferences during a session
  • Analytics cookies: To understand how users interact with our site (via Google Analytics)

We do not use cookies for tracking or advertising purposes. You can disable cookies in your browser settings, but this may affect some non-essential features. Any data stored locally (like unit preferences) remains on your device and is never transmitted to us.

4. Third-Party Services

We use trusted third-party services to operate our website:

  • Google Analytics: For anonymous traffic analysis. Google's privacy policy can be viewed at https://policies.google.com/privacy
  • Cloudflare: For DNS, security, and content delivery. Cloudflare may collect basic connection data as described in their privacy policy.
  • Unpkg and CDN services: For delivering JavaScript libraries like Cropper.js and jsPDF. These services may log standard server information.
  • Lucide Icons: For icon rendering. This service does not collect any user data.

These third parties are contractually obligated to handle data in compliance with applicable data protection laws. We recommend reviewing their privacy policies for more information.

5. Data Security

While no method of internet transmission is 100% secure, we take reasonable measures to protect our website:

  • HTTPS encryption for all connections
  • Regular security updates and monitoring
  • Minimal data collection approach
  • Client-side processing eliminates the risk of data breaches involving your images
  • No databases storing user information

Since we do not store any images or personal data, there is no risk of your information being exposed through our servers.

6. Your Rights (GDPR, CCPA and Other Laws)

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have certain rights regarding your personal data:

European Union residents (GDPR): You have the right to access, rectify, erase, and restrict processing of your personal data. Since we collect no personal data, these rights don't apply to stored information, but you can still contact us with questions.

California residents (CCPA): You have the right to request disclosure of personal data collected, request deletion, and opt-out of sale of personal information. As we collect no personal data and do not sell any information, there is nothing to disclose.

Other jurisdictions: We process minimal anonymous data in compliance with local laws. If you have specific concerns about your privacy rights, please contact us.

To exercise any rights you may have, please contact us using the information below. We will respond to all legitimate requests within 30 days.

7. Children's Privacy

ImResizer is intended for general audiences, including students of all ages. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with any information, please contact us immediately and we will take steps to remove such information.

The Service is designed to be safe for children as no data collection occurs. However, we recommend that children under 13 use the Service under adult supervision.

8. International Data Transfers

Since we do not collect or store any personal data, no international data transfers occur. Your images remain on your device and are never transmitted across borders. Anonymous analytics data may be processed by Google Analytics on servers located in various countries, in compliance with applicable data protection laws.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy occasionally to reflect changes in our practices, legal requirements, or to improve clarity. The updated version will be posted on this page with a revised "Last Updated" date. If we make material changes, we will notify you through a prominent notice on our website.

We encourage you to review this privacy policy periodically to stay informed about how we protect your privacy. Your continued use of the Service after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy.

10. Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy, our data practices, or if you would like to exercise any privacy rights, please contact us:

  • Email: contact.pandacybercafe@gmail.com
  • Response Time: Within 5-7 business days

For general inquiries, please visit our Contact page.

By using ImResizer, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this privacy policy. Your trust is important to us, and we are committed to protecting your privacy.
