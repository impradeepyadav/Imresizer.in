Welcome to ImResizer's Passport Photo Maker – your all-in-one solution for creating compliant passport, visa, and ID photos. All processing happens 100% client-side in your browser – your photos never leave your device, ensuring complete privacy.

How to Use This Tool

  1. Upload your photo: Click or drag & drop your image (JPG or PNG, max 10MB).
  2. Select your country: Choose from 50+ passport/visa presets – sizes update automatically.
  3. Adjust head position: Use the face guide to center your face properly.
  4. Change background: Click white, light blue, or use custom color picker.
  5. Fine-tune: Adjust brightness, contrast, saturation, rotate, or flip if needed.
  6. Download: Click "Create Photo" then download as JPG, PNG, or PDF.
  7. Print multiple copies: Use the print sheet generator to arrange multiple photos on one page.

📸 Photo Tips for Best Results

Use a well-lit photo with plain background. Face should be centered, eyes open, and expression neutral. Remove glasses if they cause glare. The head should occupy 70-80% of the frame – our head guide helps with positioning.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What are the requirements for passport photos?

A: Requirements vary by country but generally include: neutral expression, eyes open, plain white/light background, no shadows, no glasses or accessories, head centered and properly sized.

Q: Can I use this for visa applications?

A: Yes, our tool includes presets for US visa, Schengen visa, UK visa, and many others. Always verify with official requirements before submitting.

Q: How do I print passport photos at home?

A: Use our "Generate Print Sheet" button to arrange multiple copies on 4x6 or A4 paper. Save the sheet and print on photo paper.

Q: Is this tool really free?

A: Yes, completely free with no watermarks, no registration, and no hidden fees.

Q: Can I change background color?

A: Yes, choose from white, light blue, light gray, or any custom color using the color picker.
