About ImResizer

Last Updated: February 2026

Welcome to ImResizer

ImResizer is a free online image resizing and compression tool built specifically for students, job aspirants, and professionals who need to prepare photos for government exams, visa applications, and official documents.

Our Mission

Our mission is simple: to help you avoid photo rejection in your important applications. Every year, thousands of applications get rejected due to incorrect image size, wrong file format, or oversized files. We built ImResizer to solve this problem once and for all.

Why We Built ImResizer

As students ourselves, we struggled with complex photo requirements for exams like SSC, UPSC, IBPS, and RRB. Most photo editors are either too complicated, cost money, or don't support exact dimensions in centimeters. We wanted a tool that is:

  • Simple: Upload, adjust, download – that's it.
  • Accurate: Exact dimensions in cm, mm, inches, and pixels.
  • Secure: Your photos never leave your device (100% client-side).
  • Free: Forever free, no hidden charges, no registration.

Key Features

  • Compress to Exact KB: Target specific file sizes like 20KB, 50KB, or 100KB with precision.
  • Multiple Units: Resize in centimeters, millimeters, inches, or pixels with DPI control.
  • Exam Presets: Ready-made sizes for SSC, UPSC, IBPS, GATE, Banking, and Railway exams.
  • International Presets: US Visa, UK Passport, Schengen Visa, Japan, Canada, China, and more.
  • Name & Date Overlay: Add applicant details in DD/MM/YYYY format as required by SSC.
  • PDF Export: Create PDF documents with exact print dimensions for professional use.
  • Printable Sheets: Generate multiple copies of photos on a single page for photo studios.
  • Image Enhancements: Grayscale, signature mode, brightness, contrast, and rotation tools.
  • Crop Tool: Precise cropping with aspect ratio lock for perfect compositions.

Our Technology

ImResizer is built using modern web technologies that run entirely in your browser:

  • HTML5 Canvas: For all image processing operations
  • Cropper.js: For precise, interactive image cropping
  • jsPDF: For PDF document generation
  • Lucide Icons: For clean, professional interface icons

Key Technical Advantage: All processing happens locally on your device. No servers, no uploads, no privacy concerns. Your images never leave your computer. This makes ImResizer one of the most secure image editing tools available online.

Who We Serve

ImResizer is trusted by millions of users worldwide, including:

  • Students: Preparing photos for SSC, UPSC, Banking, Railway, and other competitive exams
  • Job Aspirants: Creating application photos for government and private sector jobs
  • Travelers: Resizing photos for US Visa, UK Passport, Schengen Visa, and other international travel documents
  • Photo Studios: Using our printable sheet feature to create multiple copies efficiently
  • Professionals: Preparing ID photos for company badges and professional certifications

Our Commitment to Privacy

Privacy is at the core of everything we do. Unlike other online image editors that upload your photos to their servers, ImResizer processes everything in your browser. This means:

  • We never see your photos
  • We cannot access your personal information
  • No data leaves your device
  • Your privacy is completely protected

For more details, please read our Privacy Policy.
